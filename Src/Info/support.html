Speedy Maths Support

Speedy maths is an app designed to improve your mental maths skills, on the home screen you can select a topic, and then select one of these 3 options:
  • Read the Tutorial
  • Play Singleplayer mode - Timed quiz
  • Play Multiplayer mode - Timed quiz but with other people at the same time
